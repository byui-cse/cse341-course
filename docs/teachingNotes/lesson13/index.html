Lesson 13 Instructor Guide


Administrative Notes

  • In your announcement each lesson, ensure that your students know what time the final project is due. On campus sections should have their final projects due at the beginning of their last class on of the lesson (when they would usually have team work time), then class time can be spent watching the presentations of all of the groups and all of their projects.
