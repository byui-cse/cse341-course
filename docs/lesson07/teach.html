Overview

The team activity assignment this lesson is to submit a project proposal. Please review the project 2 requirements below.

With the feedback provided, finalize your risk analysis and mitigation plan and submit it to I-learn.

Assignment

  1. After having completed your team activity, you should have a good idea of what a good project would be that will both meet the requirements of the course project, as well as allow you to be successful throughout the remainder of the semester.

  2. Your assignment is to create a project proposal for your project.

  3. Please use the link above to see the requirements for this project.

Topics to Include in your Project Proposal

  1. What will the app be?

  2. What will the app do?

  3. What kind of data layer/database will you incorporate?

  4. Will you choose to use a frontend JavaScript framework? If so, which? If not, why not?

  5. How will your app utilize a login system?

  6. What views will change based on roles, or logged in status?

  7. What pieces of data in your app will need to be secured? How will you demonstrate web security principles in the development of this app?

  8. What file structure and program architecture will you use for this project? Why?

  9. What are potential stretch challenges that you could implement to get 100%

  10. What are the risks involved with you being able to finish this project in a timely manner?

  11. How will you mitigate or overcome these risks?
