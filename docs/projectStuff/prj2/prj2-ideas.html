Project 2 Ideas

Overview

For the second half of the semester we will be building web applications using Node.js. You are encouraged to develop something that will be useful, or something that interests you personally.

If you cannot think of an app to create, you should ask friends, family, or co-workers to see if they have a website they need developed, or have always you to see become a reality. If you find yourself struggling to come up with something, here are a few things that other students have done to get your creative juices running regarding what kind of an application you could make.

  • sudoku games - save current game progress, store stats

  • geographical locator - plant and wildlife there, save favorites

  • family blog - family space to post updates

  • family event scheduler - used for scheduling the use of family yacht

  • journal - log in, journal, save images, etc.

  • board game store - sell board games, answer customer questions

  • board game personal collection - Digital representation of all of your games in one place

  • score keeper - Keep long term stats of who wins and who loses in any game

  • to do list - able to prioritize, share, etc.

  • team calendar - calendar that syncs across multiple accounts

  • deck builder game

  • d&d match app
