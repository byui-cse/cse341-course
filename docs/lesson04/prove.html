Overview

Assignment

  1. Create a MongoDB database for your project.

  2. Successfully perform CRUD operations.

  3. Your app should have products and the ability to add, delete and update products in the cart (Create, Read, Update, Delete).

  4. Please review rubric below to see how you'll be graded and to ensure you've completed each aspect of this assignment.

Instructor tip

If after completing the Team Activity you still feel a bit lost with MongoDB, it is okay. This video goes through many of the important concepts that you will need to know.
